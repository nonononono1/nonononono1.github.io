Summer Breezes

Pine Grove

Climbing up the rocks
the scent of Pine and Jasmine
perfumes the forest

Smores

Marshallows roasting,
chocolate flowing down onto
the golden cracker

Lotus in the Lake

Lilypads on the
lake surface, drifting until
the Lotus opens

and not a strict haiku, but

time flows away like; the melting winter snowfall; melting until gone
